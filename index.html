Welcome to Forodhani House !

Located on Shella Beach on the very spot where a watch-post once stood, guiding the boats into and out of Lamu Island; Forodhani is a beautiful beachfront house with stunning 270° sea-views. It comes with 5 professional, friendly staff, among them an excellent cook. We will do everything possible to make sure your holidays here, whether with family or friends, are both relaxing and interesting. And a little bit magic too !
